## Project Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Amazon's global e-commerce performance, providing actionable insights into sales trends, customer behavior, geographic performance, and product category effectiveness.

The `Amazon Global Dashboard.pbix` file contains the complete dynamic report and data model.

---

## üéØ Goals and Key Insights

The dashboard facilitates detailed exploration across critical business dimensions:

### 1. Sales & Revenue Analysis
- **Total Revenue & YoY Growth**: Tracks financial health and expansion pace.
- **Sales Trends**: Monthly/Quarterly revenue breakdown identifying seasonality and growth cycles.
- **Profitability**: Gross margin and net profit analysis by region and product.

### 2. Geographic Performance
- **Country/Region Comparison**: Map-based visualizations comparing sales volume and customer count.
- **Fulfillment Efficiency**: Metrics tracking shipping costs and regional delivery times.

### 3. Customer & Product Insights
- **Top Products/Categories**: Best-selling and most profitable items identification.
- **Customer Segmentation**: Repeat vs. new customer acquisition rates.
- **Rating & Review Sentiment**: Product satisfaction analysis through aggregated data.

## üìà Dashboard Features

- **Interactive Filters**: Drill down by region, product category, time period, and customer segment
